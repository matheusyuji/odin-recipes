Moscow Mule Cocktail

This classic Moscow mule recipe uses vodka, ginger beer, ice, and lime to create a refreshing cocktail that has nothing to do with Moscow or mules, but after a few sips of this vodka-based concoction, no one will care!

A Moscow mule is a vodka-based cocktail made with lime juice and ginger beer. Though the drink is associated with Russia (for obvious reasons), it's actually an American invention that dates back to the 1940s. Moscow mules are served in copper mugs because the cold metal insulates the liquid inside, keeping the cocktail cool and refreshing.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ fluid ounces vodka
  • ½ fluid ounce lime juice
  • ice cubes
  • ½ cup ginger beer
  • 1 lime wedge for garnish

Steps

  1. Pour vodka and lime juice into a mug; add ice cubes and ginger beer. Stir to combine.
  2. Drop a lime wedge into the mug for garnish.
